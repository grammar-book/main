## Exclusions

### Some words using only WITH `the`

#### Geographic things
  - `the moon`
  - `the sun`
  - `the sea`
  - `the sky`
  - `the world`
  - `the country`

#### Government services
  - `the police`
  - `the fire brigade`
  - `the army`

#### Directions
  - `the top`
  - `the bottom`
  - `the middle`
  - `the end`
  - `the left`
  - `the right`
  - `etc.`

#### Musical instruments
  - `the piano`
  - `the guitar`
  - `the trumpet`
  - `etc.`

#### Communication services
  - `the internet`
  - `the radio`

### Some words using only WITHOUT `the`

#### Meals
  - `breakfast`
  - `lunch`
  - `dinner`

#### TV
  - `television`
  - `TV (But: Can you turn off the TV)`

#### Calendar things with next/last:
  - `next/last day/week/month/year`
  - `next/last summer/winter/autumn/spring`
  - `next/last Monday/Tuesday/Wednesday/Thursday/Friday/Saturday/Sunday`
